Pistolet Original

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on December 20, 2013 in City Centre, Coffee, Drink, Eat, Sunday

The ‘pistolet’ used to be quite the hero of many bakeries. A small round bun, crisp on the outside and very light and fluffy inside. It has lost some popularity over the years (to the bigger baguette), but there’s a new eatery at Sablon, keen to restore the pistolet in all its glory.
