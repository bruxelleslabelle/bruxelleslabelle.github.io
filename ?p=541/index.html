Bel Air

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on September 25, 2011 in City Centre, Clothes |
Subscribe

I discovered Bel Air a few years ago in a multi brand store and am very happy Brussels finally has got its own shop, entirely dedicated to the brand. It only just opened, in the Louise shopping gallery.
Bel Air is a French brand. I love it for its great basics (think linen vest or t-shirt, cashmere cardigans etc), eye for detail and still affordable price tag (although some items are overpriced, I think).

The new shop is located in the Louise gallery, and the best way to find it is to walk down Avenue Louise until you’re at the COS store, then enter the gallery … it’s the second shop on the left.

Spread over two floors (connected with a steep white wooden staircase) you’ll find pants, t-shirts, sweaters, … I was charmed by the ’1984′ college sweater, finally one with my birth year on! I loved the long pullovers with sleeve pads and a visible zipper all the way down the back. And as always, felt the urgent need to get a bit more of the linen t-shirts (they have a great cut, and unlike some other French brands, keeps their shape after washing!)

3, Louise Gallery, open from Monday to Saturday, from 10.00-18.30
