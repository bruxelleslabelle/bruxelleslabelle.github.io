La Vaiselle au Kilo

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on July 15, 2011 in City Centre, Homeware |
Subscribe

The largest and most affordable range of plates, cups, glasses and cutlery is to be found at La Vaiselle au Kilo in Rue Haute.

From teenie weenie little cups to big pizza plates, and all of that in different styles. If you’re after a set of new plates, you will probably find them here.

Unlike many other kitchenware shops, this one doesn’t sell many ‘gadgets’ or funny accessories, it’s al about the basics …. well, if you can call a collection of about 50 different glasses basics, that is.

Cake stands, margarita glasses, metal ‘reserved’ tags, inspiring stuff for dinner parties. I hardly ever walk past the shop without a quick peak inside or an inspecting glance at the window.

 

124-126, Rue Haute, open every day
