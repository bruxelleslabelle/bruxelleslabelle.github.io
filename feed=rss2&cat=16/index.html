There are so many styles and brands to choose from. You need time and patience to shop there though.

The biggest DOD is located near the Madou metro station. I usually head down to minus one first, to check out the shoes. If you’re lucky, you can score a great pair of Balmain, DKNY or Valentino shoes (although that never happened to me unfortunately, as my size is always gone).

DOD often has sample sale collections in as well. Good news if you have a standard size, which means you’ll fit in all of it: Fine, American Vintage etc. They also have large collections by Superdry, DKNY, Bellerose etc … and a rack with designers stuff by Missoni and Valentino. (They have loads of brands I don’t know too, spread over 4 (!) floors!). The top floor has underwear: Princesse Tam Tam, Chantelle, Passionata, Calvin Klein .. all at reduced prices.

DOD has shops for men and children as well.

Besides the big shop near Madou, you’ll find DOD in the Bailli (more designer brands and lingerie, kids, and men) area too as well as near Bascule (kids and shoes).

Check their website and apply for a ‘loyalty card’, which grants you a ten percent discount on all your purchases.

44, Chaussée de Louvain
64, Rue du Bailli
714, Chaussée de Waterloon
