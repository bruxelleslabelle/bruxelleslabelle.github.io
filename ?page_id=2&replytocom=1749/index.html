About me

I love Brussels and this blog will show you all my preferred spots in the city. Whether it is an established shop or a new bar, it doesn’t matter, as long as I truly enjoyed being there.

Exploring the city is one of my favourite activities. As soon as I was old enough to venture out on my own, that’s all I wanted to do: see things, visit new places, wander about …

I’m lucky that so many years later, venturing out to discover new places in Brussels became part of my job as a freelance lifestyle writer for several city magazines.

As you’ll discover, I have a soft spot for cosy coffee places, cute lunch eateries, homeware shops and fashion. As I live in the ‘south’ of Brussels, a lot of the places mentioned will be from this area too…

I hope you enjoy reading what ‘Bruxelles la Belle’ has got to offer!
