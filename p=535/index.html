LOL

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on September 22, 2011 in Clothes, Uccle |
Subscribe

Despite its rather strange name (LOL refers to the nick name of owner Laurence, not ‘laughing out loud’), this boutique is a must-visit for girls and women looking for top brands such as Le Mont Saint Michel, Filles à Papa, Carven, J Brand etc. 

The multi brand shop is located in the posh area of Vert Chasseur in Uccle, and is a great addition to the existing range of shops: Sandro, Stella Forest, etc.

LOL focuses on popular brands, that are hard to find in Brussels. I loved all the knitwear by Mont Saint Michel, the crazy sweaters by Les Filles à Papa, the refined skirts by Carven, and the shredded t-shirts by Zoe’s Tees. I was quite pleased to see they had some MM6 by Margiela as well!

Laurence is very well aware she’s selling rather pricy stuff, that’s why she’s also stocking a range of vintage dresses. She gets them from London or Paris, or brings them along after her travels.

Next season, Acne will be added to the list of brands, and OPI nail varnish will be available as well. Yeay!

1087 Chaussée de Waterloo, open from Monday until Saturday, from 10.00-18.30
