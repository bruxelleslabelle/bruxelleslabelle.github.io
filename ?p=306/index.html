Atchoum

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on July 14, 2011 in City Centre, Homeware |
Subscribe
I never deliberately go vintage shopping, it always just happens. On a stroll through Les Marolles, I popped into Atchoum – a shop specialised in beads and pearls – and left with a vintage chair to go with my desk.

Yes yes, a shop that sells beads and everything you need to make necklaces turns out to have a ‘vintage department’ as well. In the basement of the shop you can find treasures from the past decades. Typically orange lamps and plates, chairs, cute tables, a china tea set …

I discovered the shop a while ago. On my first visit I found a beautiful magazine rack. On my second visit I bought a vintage chair (at 30% off, as they do sales as well).

As with most of the vintage shops in the area, bring your patience and cash money!

20, Rue des Renards, open Tuesday to Sunday, 10.30-16.30
