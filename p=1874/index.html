AUB.SVP

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on October 27, 2013 in City Centre, Coffee, Drink, Eat |
Subscribe

Located in the entrance hall of a former movie theatre, this small eatery is a true gem. Breakfast, lunch, afternoon and after-work treats like you haven’t seen before (or at least, not in Brussels).

The place is small and cozy, and the same goes for the menu. It’s divided in four parts: before 12, after 12, a veggie option and dessert, all with about five different suggestions. But don’t be fooled, choosing is difficult.

When I arrived around 11.30, there were a few people eating already. The place smelled of delicious bread, which is AUB.SVP’s proud protagonist. Every dish comes with a special kind of homemade bread.

I couldn’t choose between the selection of cheese served with sourdough, the lemon brioche with quark or the poached eggs with bacon bread (the homemade granola didn’t make the short list, as I was after something more savoury). I picked the eggs and bacon bread and went for a glass of boskop apple juice to go with it.

The eggs came served on spinach leaves, perfectly poached and still slightly runny, with fresh cracked pepper and warm bacon bread. Needless to say, it was a delight.

As it was nearing 12 o’ clock by the time we finished our plates, the very friendly cook/host checked if we cared for something else. He just finished blending his soup of the day, so we ordered a bowl of that too. Served in a nice looking glass, the courgette soup was seasoned with some coconut and ginger. Delicious too.

The bill came at 11 euro per person, which is a more than honest price for what we ate.

The after 12 menu features amongst other Simmental steak and a fish of the day dish, vegetarians can feast on salads or a grilled veggies puff pastry parcel, and the dessert list includes cheese with wine bread, rhubarb and cream and bread pudding.

I’m already looking forward to my next visit!

36 Rue de L’Ecuyer, open from Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday between 07.30-15.00, on Thursdays also from 18.00-22.00 and on Saturdays between 11.00-15.00
