Tea & Eat

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on November 8, 2011 in City Centre, Drink, Eat |
Subscribe

I took a different route to work, and walked into Tea & Eat to grab my lunch: a very pretty tearoom/grocery store.

A whole wall covered in big tea tins, a open cupboard bulking with spices and an large counter with bright lemonades, salads, sandwiches and cakes. Sounds great, right?

Tea & Eat has got plenty of breakfast, brunch and lunch options to eat in the pretty interior, but as I’m always on the go, I opted for a wrap and a stylish looking bottle of lemon and mint infused water (I forgot the brand, it was something American and had ‘mint’ in it).

I was quite tempted to grab a box of the mixed pepper corns from all over the world as well, but they were a bit pricy … Great idea for a gift though, as are most of the biscuits, teas, spices …

Quite looking forward to a next visit, especially as I didn’t have to queue to get served, which is a big plus in the busy Stéphanie area.

121-124 Rue de Stassart, open every day
