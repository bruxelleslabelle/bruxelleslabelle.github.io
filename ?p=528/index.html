Natural Caffè

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on September 20, 2011 in Drink, Eat, Ixelles |
Subscribe

Illy coffee in all its glory … that’s my cup of tea err .. coffee!! The Natural Caffè ‘Illy’ used to be on my way to work .. no longer now, but I don’t mind a little detour for a hot cup of Illy.

I quite like the concept of this little barrista/eatery. Coffee in any shape or form, homemade cake and a selection of organic salads, sandwiches and desserts. Part of the decor, but also for sale: a large selection of vintage looking coffee makers by Francis.

Know one thing: it’s a rather pricy place. Simple coffee to go costs 2 euro (that’s ok), but a large muesli bar costs 2.5 euro, salads starts at 7, sandwiches around 5. It’s very easy to get carried away and order a lot more than you intended. All those times I did let go of my original plan of ‘one coffee’, I felt disappointed with the flavourless salad or the chewy ciabatta. The big muesli bar (at the register), never let me down, though.

196, Avenue Louise, open every day (more addresses in Brussels, but this is ‘my local’)
