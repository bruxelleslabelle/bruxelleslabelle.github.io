Forever 21

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on July 29, 2011 in City Centre, Clothes, Shoes |
Subscribe

Rue Neuve is paved with chain stores, so I usually avoid the area on a shopping spree … but when Forever 21 opened its doors, I just had to have a look to see what the fuss was all about.

It’s been announced for months in a row: THE American chain store would come to Europe and open its first store in the Benelux down Rue Neuve. The shop is located where Mexx used to be, but the first thing you’ll notice once inside is that it is so much bigger than the old store. HUGE! 3.000m2 to be precise, which is the size of a small football field.

Every bit of space is stacked with clothes, women only. Why? Because there was not enough space to add the men’s collection as well .. Hard to believe, but then again, the Forever 21 assortment is endless.

The style is quite young, with cute t-shirts, short skirts and dresses, a very affordable denim range, a boudoir style underwear department, shoes and lots of accessories.

The prices are unbelievably cheap. But in exchange, you’ll by a jumper made from polyester rather than cotton and shoes from ‘leatherette’ rather than leather …

With 19 fitting room, the shop is ready for the crowd!

15, Rue Neuve, open from Monday until Saturday, 09.30-19.00 (>20.00 on Fridays)
