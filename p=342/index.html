Francis Ferent

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on July 22, 2011 in Clothes, Ixelles |
Subscribe

I love multi-brand stores because of their diverse range of styles and designers. Francis Ferent on Avenue Louise in particular, sells a few of my favourite fashion creators: Sonia Rykiel, Vanessa Bruno and Marc Jacobs.

The shop on Avenue Louise is very large, and sells clothes and shoes for men and women. There’s one dedicated to shoes only, at the Louise Gallery, where you’ll find another men/women one as well.

I usually go to the big Ferent, where I head straight to the back to discover the new clothes by Vanessa Bruno or bags by Marc Jacobs. I always have a peak at their gadgets as well, they have amazing scented candles, great coffee table books, cute notebooks etc.

The shoes at the front are worth a look too, although very expensive. But during the sales, aha, that’s a different story: 50% off as of week 2…

One remark though, I think this shop should try to loosen up a bit. They have great designers in store, but always pick the most chique items.  Where’s the rock & roll?

60, Avenue Louise, closed on Sundays
