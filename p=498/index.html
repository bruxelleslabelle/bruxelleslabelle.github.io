The Hairy Canary

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on September 4, 2011 in City Centre, Drink |
Subscribe

Carpets every where, football on tv and Guinness on draught… The Hairy Canary is as real as a British pub can be! 

Of all the pubs in Brussels, this one is quite ‘special’ to me, as it is the place where I had my first Magners, about 4 years ago. Nothing too exciting, I know, but back then I was rather impressed by the large bottle of cider and the fact it came served over ice.

Go and have a drink on an evening during the week, you’ll have a good time. It’s usually a lot quieter on weekends, so the atmosphere is not exactly the same.

Cheers!

12, Rue Archimède
