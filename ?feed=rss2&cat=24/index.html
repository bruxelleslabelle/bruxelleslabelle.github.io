And even better, home to the one and only Godiva Factory outlet shop with chocolate at a very sweet price.

First of all, the shop is tiny. Living room tiny. But no worries, there’s enough to choose from: mixed chocolate boxes sold at half of the original price, chocolate covered biscuits, chocolate spreads, and even the chocolate hot and cold drinks!

There’s nothing wrong with all the items sold. They’re not past or close to the sell-by-date, the biscuits are not broken in a million pieces and the spreads have not been opened before. The range is about the same as in a regular Godiva boutique, just a lot cheaper.

I left the store with two boxes of chocolate biscuits and a tin of Godiva Early Grey tea (I didn’t even know they did tea!). The receipt stated 8 euro .. Now, how sweet is that?
