De Banier

Posted by Bruxelles la Belle on June 24, 2011 in City Centre, Homeware, Kids |
Subscribe

I recently rediscovered the joys of this shop. When I was a kid, I visited the shop often, as it belonged to the ‘youth movement’ I was part of. It’s the perfect place to get everything for some arts & crafts.

You’ll for instance find ready-made greeting cards, as well as everything you need to make your own. Cardboard, felt, pearls, all sorts of paper, scissors, special kinds of glue, ribbons, glitters, stickers, ….

It’s a great place to get some inspiration, browse and buy DIY books, and get started!

85, Rue Marché au Charbon, open Monday until Saturday, between 10.00-13.00 and 14.00-18.00
